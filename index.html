About the Game

Eco-Frog is a 2D educational game designed to promote environmental awareness and proper waste segregation. Players clean up trash, sort different types of waste, and answer quick trivia that reinforces eco-friendly habits. Through fun and interactive gameplay, Eco-Frog helps young learners build ecological responsibility in an enjoyable and memorable way.

Gameplay

How to Play: Eco Frog is a game with an important message. As a frog, your mission is to defend your home the river from enemies: the trash that pollutes it, such as banana peels, cans, and other garbage. Using your skills, you must clean up the river and segregate the trash in the trash bins to restore its beauty and health. Game Mechanics

1. Player: Controls a frog that moves and jumps around.

2. Attack: Shoots water bullets to destroy enemies.

3. Objective: Defeat all pollution monsters and clean the environment.

4. Scoring: Gain points for every enemy defeated.

5. Health: Frog loses health when hit or touched by enemies.
